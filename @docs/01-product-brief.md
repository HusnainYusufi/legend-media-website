### 01 — Product Brief

**Name**: Leaf Solutions — A showcase website for a digital solutions agency specializing in scalable web and app development.

**Platform**: Web only (Next.js App Router)

**Primary Goal**: Create an award-winning, conversion-focused marketing website that demonstrates technical excellence while showcasing our portfolio and expertise.

**Core Sections**
- **Hero**: Impactful landing with animated tagline and primary CTAs
- **About**: Company overview with strategic pillars (Think, Analyze, Create)
- **Stats**: Social proof with animated counters (500+ projects, 450+ clients, etc.)
- **Projects**: Portfolio showcase with detailed case studies (Sticha App, Ganna Healing)
- **Testimonials**: Client reviews with infinite scroll carousel
- **CTA**: Conversion-focused contact section
- **Footer**: Complete contact information and company details

**Target Audience**
- **Primary**: Businesses seeking custom web/app development (SMBs, startups, scale-ups)
- **Secondary**: Individuals with innovative ideas needing technical partners

**Goals**
- Establish credibility through stunning design and smooth interactions
- Showcase portfolio projects with compelling visual storytelling
- Drive conversions through strategic CTAs and booking flows
- Create a memorable brand experience that reflects technical excellence

**Non-Goals (for v1)**
- No backend integration (static/client-side only for now)
- No authentication or user accounts
- No real-time features or complex state management
- No payment processing (external booking/contact forms)

**Brand Theme**
- **Primary**: `#10B981` (Leaf Green - vibrant, growth-focused)
- **Secondary**: `#064E3B` (Dark Green - professional, trustworthy)
- **Accent**: `#34D399` (Light Green - highlights, hovers)
- **Neutral Dark**: `#0F172A` (Slate 900 - text, backgrounds)
- **Neutral Light**: `#F8FAFC` (Slate 50 - light backgrounds)
- **Text**: `#0F172A` (primary), `#64748B` (secondary/muted)

**Voice & Style**
- Professional yet approachable. Clear, concise English. Solution-focused messaging.
- Confident but not boastful. Technical expertise expressed through quality, not jargon.
- Modern, clean aesthetic with purposeful motion design.

**Award-Winning Design Philosophy**
- **World-Class UX**: Buttery-smooth Lenis scrolling for exceptional browsing experience
- **Motion Excellence**: Scroll-triggered animations, parallax effects, magnetic interactions
- **Visual Impact**: Stunning layouts with dynamic imagery, creative compositions, polished aesthetics
- **Performance-First**: 60fps animations while delivering rich, interactive experiences
- **Attention to Detail**: Every element, timing curve, and transition carefully crafted
- **Innovation**: Push boundaries with modern techniques worthy of Awwwards recognition
- **Storytelling**: Progressive disclosure that guides users through a compelling narrative

**Design Language**
- **Professional Minimalism**: Clean layouts, generous white space, focused content hierarchy
- **Typography**: Inter font family (400, 500, 600, 700 weights) for clarity and modernity
- **Geometric Patterns**: Clean lines, subtle gradients, purposeful use of cards and containers
- **Growth & Innovation**: Upward movement animations, expanding reveals, transformative transitions
- **Trust Building**: High-quality imagery, testimonial cards, stat counters, professional polish

**Technical Excellence**
- Lighthouse score 95+ across all metrics
- Smooth 60fps animations throughout
- Responsive design (mobile-first approach)
- SEO optimized with proper meta tags and structured data
- Accessible (WCAG AA compliance)

**Accessibility**
- Color contrast AA or better
- Focus states for keyboard navigation
- Screen reader labels (aria-*)
- Semantic HTML structure
- Large tap targets (minimum 44x44px)
- Reduced motion support for users with vestibular disorders

**Key Differentiators**
- Showcase real, impressive projects with detailed case studies
- Demonstrate technical capability through the website itself
- Fast, smooth, delightful interactions throughout
- Clear conversion paths without aggressive sales tactics
